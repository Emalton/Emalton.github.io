Our mission is to provide the staffing to help farms grow ethically sourced produce using their cutting edge hydroponic techniques.

About Us

JSK Growers is a small agricultural staffing group based out of western Wisconsin. We specialize in providing the man power to help farms grow sustainable, year-round produce using hydroponics. Our goal is to help provide extra sets of hands to our clients so there are never a sortage of workers to help harvest crop.

Hydroponic Benefits

No Soil Degradation

Hydroponic technologies result in no soil degradation.

Irrigation

Irrigation systems provides the perfect nutrients to our client's plants.

Climate Controlled

Greenhouses are climate controlled, and house bees are busy pollinating our client's crops!
