About Us

JSK Growers is a small farming group based out of western Wisconsin. We specialize in growing sustainable, year-round produce using hydroponics. Our expert growers has a degree in hydroponic farming and has mastered the ability to grow crops year around. Our goal is to feed the community and show others how to do it, so there will never be a shortage of food in the future.

Hydroponic Benefits

No Soil Degradation

Our hydroponic technologies result in no soil degradation.

Irrigation

Our custom irrigation system provides the perfect nutrients to our plants.

Climate Controlled

Our greenhouses are climate controlled, and our in-house bees are busy pollinating our crops!

Phase Two
